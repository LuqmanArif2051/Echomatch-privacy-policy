EchoMatch Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

1. Overview

EchoMatch is a location-based voice and social discovery application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users regarding their information.

By using EchoMatch, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Display name
  • Email address
  • Age range
  • Gender
  • Profile photos and gallery images
  • Optional profile information, including location, height, religion, relationship status, zodiac sign, and family preferences
  • Voice echoes and recordings
  • Chat messages
  • Likes, reports, and other interactions

Information Collected Automatically

  • Approximate or precise location (depending on permissions and feature usage)
  • Timestamps related to posts, likes, replies, and messages
  • Firebase Authentication identifiers
  • Push notification tokens
  • Device and application interaction information necessary for app functionality, security, and notifications

3. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Display profiles and social features
  • Enable map-based echo discovery
  • Support messaging, likes, replies, notifications, and moderation
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Maintain platform security
  • Prevent abuse and fraud
  • Investigate reports and policy violations
  • Improve features and overall service performance

4. Location Information

Location services are an important part of EchoMatch.

Location information may be used to:

  • Display nearby echoes
  • Attach echoes to places
  • Support location-based discovery features
  • Enforce location-related feature rules
  • Show echoes on the map when Map Visibility is enabled

EchoMatch does not display a user's live device location as a continuously tracked map marker.

Location information may also be used to:

  • Center the map around your area
  • Load nearby content
  • Associate newly created echoes with places

Before location-based features are used, users are asked for the required permissions through their device operating system.

Users may:

  • Disable location access through device settings
  • Turn off map visibility within the app
  • Stop sharing location-based content at any time

Some features may not function properly when location access is disabled.

5. Voice Content and Media

Voice echoes, recordings, profile photos, gallery images, and other uploaded content may be stored and processed so they can be displayed, shared, delivered, and moderated within the service.

Public or map-visible echoes may be discoverable by other users based on app settings and feature availability.

6. Profile Visibility

Depending on app features and user settings, other users may be able to view:

  • Display name
  • Age range
  • Gender
  • Profile photo
  • Selected profile information
  • Shared gallery photos

While EchoMatch may offer limited visibility or privacy controls, complete anonymity cannot be guaranteed.

7. Notifications

EchoMatch may send in-app and push notifications regarding:

  • Likes
  • Match activity
  • Messages
  • Feature updates
  • Safety-related notifications

Notification delivery may use Firebase Cloud Messaging and associated device identifiers.

8. Data Storage and Service Providers

EchoMatch uses trusted third-party providers, including Google Firebase, to support:

  • User authentication
  • Database services
  • File storage
  • Push notifications
  • Application infrastructure

Information may be processed and stored on systems operated by these providers.

9. Sharing of Information

EchoMatch does not sell personal information.

We may share information:

  • With service providers that operate and support the app
  • To investigate abuse, fraud, or safety concerns
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid government requests
  • During a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or transfer of business assets

10. Safety, Reporting, and Moderation

To help maintain a safe environment, EchoMatch may review and retain relevant information when safety concerns arise or reports are submitted.

11. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, provide features, resolve disputes, enforce policies, and comply with legal obligations.

12. Your Choices and Rights

  • Edit profile information
  • Update privacy and visibility settings
  • Delete echoes and content
  • Block other users
  • Manage notification permissions through device settings
  • Stop using the service at any time

13. Children's Privacy

EchoMatch is intended only for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

14. Security

We use reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards to protect user information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

15. International Use

Information may be processed and stored in jurisdictions where privacy laws differ from those in your location.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates become effective when published on this page.

17. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@echomatch.app
